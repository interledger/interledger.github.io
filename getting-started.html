Getting Started

Sending Your First Interledger Payment

Follow our tutorials to start sending Interledger payments in under 3 minutes.

First, follow "Using Moneyd to Join the ILP Testnet" to get connected to the Interledger testnet. The "Testnet of Testnets," as we call it, connects different blockchain testnets together. We use it to test new protocols and tools in the Interledger stack.

Next, follow "SPSP: Simple Payment Setup Protocol" to send your first Interledger payment.

Congratulations, you've sent your first Interledger payment!
